Welcome to the Wisconsin Falconers Association

Falconry is the taking of wild quarry in its natural state and habitat using a trained bird of prey. The Wisconsin Falconers Association is a group of dedicated individuals who share the same views in the conservation, ethics and welfare of our birds of prey. We share ideas which enhance the care, welfare and training of our birds.

2015 WFA Picnic LOCATION CHANGE

WFA Picnic 2015

Location has changed due to storm damage.

Astico Park will not be open in time for our picnic. We have moved it to Derge Park which is 5 miles north of Beaver Dam on the west side of the lake. We will be in shelter 1, the Quonset. This holds about 60 people so it is rain or shine. This is about 20 miles north of the Astico Park location. The address is: N8379 County Road CP – Beaver Dam, WI

11 am start time. Free admission! The WFA will be providing grilled hamburgers and hotdogs as well as chips, soda, bottled water, paper plates and plastic utensils. Bringing a dish to pass (could be desert) is very much welcomed and appreciated but not required. Electricity is available in this shelter if needed. Birds are welcome! Dogs are also welcome if they are bird friendly! All animals must kept on leash and be cleaned up after of course! Your own lawn chairs are optional. Bring your kids and grandkids. Lots of room to run and a playground area nearby.

All paid WFA members who attend will also receive a WFA lapel pin.

 

Here is a link with information about the park with maps and directions.

http://www.co.dodge.wi.us/index.aspx?page=428

Hope to see everyone there.
